# Disclaimer

This project provides an AI-powered meeting summarization system using machine learning models including OpenAI Whisper for speech recognition and transformer models (BART/T5) for text summarization. While we strive for accuracy, please be aware of the following:

## Important Notices

- **Accuracy**: The generated summaries and transcripts may contain errors or inaccuracies due to limitations in AI technology, audio quality, accents, or background noise.
- **Not Legal Advice**: This tool is not intended for legal proceedings, medical records, or any critical applications where accuracy is paramount.
- **Data Privacy**: Uploaded audio files are processed locally or on the server. Please ensure you have permission to process and store any audio content.
